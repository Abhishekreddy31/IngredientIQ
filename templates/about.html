About IngredientIQ

Know what's in your food, cosmetics, and pet products

What is IngredientIQ?

IngredientIQ is a powerful tool designed to help you understand what's in the products you use and consume every day. By simply scanning a barcode or taking a photo of an ingredient list, you can quickly get detailed information about the ingredients in your products.

Our Data Sources

IngredientIQ leverages multiple open databases to provide comprehensive product information:

  • Open Food Facts - A collaborative, free and open database of food products from around the world
  • Open Beauty Facts - For cosmetics and personal care products
  • Open Pet Food Facts - For pet food products

We also use advanced Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology to extract ingredient information directly from product packaging when database information is not available.

Privacy & Security

We respect your privacy. IngredientIQ does not store any personal information. Images you upload for OCR processing are used only for that purpose and are not permanently stored.

Mobile & Desktop Compatible

IngredientIQ is designed to work seamlessly on both mobile devices and desktop computers. You can use our barcode scanner on your mobile device or manually enter barcodes on any platform.

Community Contribution

IngredientIQ is made better by community contributions. If you find a product that's not in our database, you can help by adding it. Your contributions help make our database more comprehensive for everyone.
